Part 2 – Chesterton

This is the second of four pages that take you on virtual tour of the river Cam in Cambridge, from Jesus Lock to Bait's Bite Lock. This stretch of water is the scene each year of Cambridge's famous bumping races, and of a host of College events such as the Fairbairn Cup. A rowing map of the Cam and various facts and figures are also available.

The photographs were taken by Mat Hubbard on 17 July 1997.
