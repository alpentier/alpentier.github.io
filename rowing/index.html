Racing

I rowed competitively for Churchill College Boat Club and Cambridge '99 Rowing Club from 1996 until 2004. Take a look at results and photographs from our races around the UK:

  • 1996-1997: Michaelmas, Lent, May
  • 1997-1998: Michaelmas, Lent, May
  • 1998-1999: Michaelmas, Lent, May
  • 2000-2001: Summer
  • 2001-2002: Winter, Summer
  • 2002-2003: Winter, Summer
  • 2003-2004: Winter, Summer

Since 2005, I have raced irregularly for Cambridge '99, Churchill College Shovellers, X-Press and now Rob Roy.

  • Twilight: 2005, 2006, 2008, 2009, 2010

Coaching

I began coaching crews from Churchill College Boat Club in 1998. I have been a regular coach of the men's and women's First VIII crews since 2002.

Cambridge rowing information

Rowing in Cambridge takes place on a twisting three mile stretch of the river Cam to the north of the city. The river is the site of some of the world's most exciting racing.
