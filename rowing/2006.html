Racing

X-Press Head – Mixed 4+ finished 34th

We entered a mixed crew of Churchill old-timers in the X-Press Head in Cambridge on 17 June. As the only active rower, Claudia contributed significantly more than 25% of the propulsive effect. Our time of 11'59" was good for 34th place out of 48 crews, which was both first and last in our category. As ever, we then proceeded to the Cambridge Blue for the hog roast and beer.

Crew: Angie McConnell (bow), Vanessa Mitchell, David Sampson, Claudia Kunze (stroke), Phil Cowans (cox).
