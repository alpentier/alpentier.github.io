The Churchill College Boat Club Alumni Association is the organisation for former members and friends of Churchill College Boat Club. Some reasons you might like to join are:

  • You want to hear about occasional alumni events (races, outings, dinners, etc) in Cambridge.
  • You want to keep in touch with other Churchill rowers, past and present.
  • You would appreciate some help or advice when looking for a rowing club to join permanently or to go for a paddle when travelling.
  • You are interested in helping the Club by coaching or contributing to the Equipment Fund.
