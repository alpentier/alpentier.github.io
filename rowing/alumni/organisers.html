News

  • The date of the dinner is confirmed as Saturday 16 June.
  • Through Annette Tattersall of the Churchill Alumni Office, we have secured a block of 40 rooms at a preferential rate in the Møller Centre.
  • On-line information, an on-line booking form and a printable booking form are available.
  • Eddie, David and Jack have identified key contacts from the '60s, '70s, '80s and '90s. These people will spread the word to others from their era.
  • We are working with Annette to compile a list of postal addresses for past captains from the May Boards.
