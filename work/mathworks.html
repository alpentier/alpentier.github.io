I joined MathWorks in 2000. I am a Principal Consultant and the UK Consulting Manager.

After starting out in the automotive and aerospace industries, I have branched out into transporation, retail, and financial services. My current areas of interest include:

  • Application and toolbox development
  • Data analytics
  • Modeling, control and optimization of dynamic systems
  • Model-based calibration of automotive powertrains

I have worked with many clients, including: John Deere, Mercedes-Benz, Tesco, National Australia Bank, Jaguar Land Rover, BAE Systems, Caterpillar, BHP, Gatwick Airport, Ferrari, McLaren, DSTO, Ford, HSBC, Toyota, Airbus.

With Ben Tordoff, I developed the popular GUI Layout Toolbox to support creation of sophisticated MATLAB user interfaces that resize gracefully.
